训练注意事项：

【报名】每次训练前请大家在下方报名训练("Sign Up" Button)

【个人信息注册表】（第一次训练前必须填，整个赛季填过一次即可）https://forms.gle/QqhtmaxHfL2hxxF59

【Waiver】（第一次训练前必须填，整个赛季填过一次即可）(下水训练过的请选Renewing club member,未下过水的请选New club member): https://form.jotform.com/250479064670158

【时间】请大家提前20分钟到达，预留充足的时间找车位、热身。

【停车】旁边的Metropolis已经涨价了，周五是$13，周末是$9，建议大家每次可以用SpotAngels App提前寻找附近便宜车位。

【Carpool拼车】需要carpool去训练的队友请在群里直接求助；希望有车且顺路的队友能伸出援手👋

【👗🧥】训练中衣服可能会被打湿，如有需要请大家带好可以更换的衣服，以防着凉感冒😷

【运动饮料和水】防止低血糖和脱水。

【防晒用品】防晒霜、防晒服、帽子、墨镜等。有时候训练会非常非常晒，2小时的训练可能就会造成晒伤，请做好防晒工作，永葆20岁的肌肤状态。

【防脱落】戴眼镜的同学小心划船时眼镜掉到水里，可以用眼镜绳来加固。

【防滑落】手机和车钥匙等十分建议放到船上的dry bag里面。以前有人在上船的第一分钟就给水神贡献了一个手机。


一对一龙舟技巧指导

所有新手以及想提高龙舟技术的队员请来参加一对一指导，时间为每次训练开始前的半小时，希望所有新手都可以参加至少4次的一对一指导。


报名水上训练请注意：

🌟在船上时请不要踩踏大家坐的bench

🌟因为平衡船的需要，每次水上训练都需要在下面的报名表上填上你的名字，不报名的队员不能临时过来训练。不能来训练的队员也请及时去掉你的名字，把训练机会留给别的队友。

🌟报名截止时间是每次训练的前一天晚上十点，例如本周日报名的截止时间是周六晚上十点。教练和队长会需要提前安排line-up。八点之后报名或者临时不能来训练，请私信朱睿智或者李悦明。

🌟报名时选择想要划左边还是右边。


龙舟运动中的【非必需品】，但是有了他们会让你的龙舟体验更丝滑，装备党们可以置备起来了：

【坐垫】- 防止磨屁股。往常会用剪开的瑜伽垫作为坐垫，分发的龙舟坐垫请大家每次训练自行携带。

【划船手套】- 防止手打滑。

【水鞋】- 推荐用于水上运动的鞋子（参考1，参考2，参考3）没有的话拖鞋也可以。温馨提示：穿跑鞋的话，鞋子湿了之后会一直一直很臭🤢

Training Reminders:

[Sign-up] Please make sure to sign up below (click the "Sign Up" button) before each training session.

[Registration Form] (Required for first-time paddlers, only once for the season) https://forms.gle/QqhtmaxHfL2hxxF59

[Waiver] (Required for first-time paddlers, only once for the season) (If you've participated Storm boat practice before, select "Renewing club member". New members, select "New club member"): https://form.jotform.com/250479064670158

[Arrival] Please arrive 10 minutes early to allow enough time for parking and warm-up.

[Parking] The nearby Metropolis garage has increased prices: $13 on Fridays, $9 on weekends. We recommend using the SpotAngels App to find cheaper parking nearby.

[Carpool] If you need a ride, please ask in the group chat. If you have a car and can help, please volunteer! 👋

[Clothing] Your clothes may get wet during training. Bring extra clothes if needed to avoid catching a cold. 😷

[Drinks] Bring sports drinks and water to prevent low blood sugar and dehydration.

[Sun Protection] Use sunscreen, sun-protective clothing, hats, and sunglasses. Training sessions can be very sunny—2 hours can easily cause sunburn. Please take sun protection seriously to keep your skin healthy.

[Glasses Safety] If you wear glasses, secure them with a strap to prevent them from falling into the water.

[Phone/Keys Safety] It is highly recommended to put your phone and car keys in a dry bag on the boat. Some have lost their phone in the first minute on the boat before.


1-on-1 Dragon Boat Skills Training

All beginners and anyone wanting to improve their dragon boat skills are welcome to join 1-on-1 coaching. This takes place 30 minutes before each training session. We encourage all beginners to join at least 4 times.


Important Reminders for On-Water Training Sign-up:

🌟 Please do not step on the benches on the boat.

🌟 For the balance of the boat, everyone must sign up using the form below. Those not signed up cannot join the training at the last minute. If you can’t attend, please remove your name in time to free the slot for others.

🌟 The sign-up deadline is 10pm the day before each training (e.g., for Sunday training, the deadline is Saturday 10pm). Coaches and captains need time to set the line-up. If you need to sign up after the deadline or can’t come last-minute, please contact Ruizhi Zhu or Yueming Li directly.

🌟 Please indicate if you want to paddle on the left or right side when signing up.


Here are some optional items that can make your dragon boat experience even smoother (not required):

[Seat Pad] To protect your seat. We often use cut yoga mats; please bring your dragon boat seat pad for every session if you have one.

[Paddling Gloves] To prevent hands from slipping.

[Water Shoes] Recommended for water sports (option 1, option 2, option 3). If you don’t have these, sandals are fine. (Pro tip: If you wear running shoes, they’ll get soaked and stay very smelly!) 🤢
